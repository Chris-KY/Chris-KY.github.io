About

I’m a robotics engineer specialized in Intelligent Systems from Sorbonne University, with hands-on experience in autonomous firefighting robots, Smart Factory 4.0 projects, and drone fleets for pollution measurement. My background spans Python, ROS 2, neural networks, computer vision, and signal processing, enabling me to approach challenges from a broad, innovative perspective..

I’m a curious individual who thrives on learning from diverse experiences. Having worked in various types of organizations—ranging from Airbus to Shark Robotics—and traveled extensively, including humanitarian missions in South Sudan, I’ve developed independence, adaptability, and a global outlook that fuel my drive to explore and innovate.
